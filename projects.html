Confronting Misogyny

This is a documentary about activist and feminist Wonyun Lee who spoke up about the state of misogyny in South Korea and confronted her online harassers. Lee has published a book about Megalia, a South Korean online forum and feminist movement that began in 2015. I was the director, producer, editor, co-animator, and music composer of this film.
